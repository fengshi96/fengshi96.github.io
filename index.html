Willkommen! I'm a theorist in condensed matter physics and quantum information. I studied and worked in Xi'an, Los Angeles, Columbus and Munich. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow working with Quantum matter / quantum information theory group at Technical University of Munich, Germany; formerly a graduate student of condensed matter theory in Prof. Trivedi's group at The Ohio State University, USA. I am interested in understanding quantum many-body systems, particularly frustrated quantum systems such as quantum spin liquids, topological entanglement and novel quantum dynamics therein, and machine learning methods for many-body systems.
